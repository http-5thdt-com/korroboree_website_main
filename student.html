Why We Exist:

You will need more than what conventional education is providing you. School success does not guarantee or reflect life success. The challenges that life will test you with, cannot be learned from a textbook and not in an education system that continues to compare and grade you in standardised ways.

A “fixed mindset” assumes that your character, intelligence, and creative ability are fixed and you can’t change in any meaningful way. We will provide you with learning tools and resources that build in you, a growth mindset. As you use the tools and resources you will correctly believe that you can control your skills and abilities based on your effort and learning.

How We Will Make a Difference
WE WILL-

We are here to provide you with tools and resources that will help you create the best version of yourself. Tools that help you explore and develop your own capacity for creativity and positive mind habits. You were born with your own dreams, talents and interests. We are here to help you develop the belief, confidence and develop the mindset to make those dreams come true.

The world needs you to dream of something great and then to pursue it with all your head and your heart. We want to encourage you to use your gifts and your talents to their fullest extent. We will give you tools that will help you connect to your dream, commit to your dream and work to the realisation of your dream with step-by-step planning and learning tools. For you to be a self-leader you must have learning autonomy and choice. Korroboree provides you with both.

What We Offer

We will provide you with blogs, videos, journals and courses that will motivate you to learn, create and innovate. You will have a range of self paced learning platforms that you can subscribe to based on what it is that you are keen to learn. You take the control and reins of your learning, happiness, fulfillment and success.
