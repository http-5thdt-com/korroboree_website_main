AGNELORAJESH ATHAID


Co-Founder:-Korroboree | Chairman: St. Angelos VNCT Ventures | Author, Social Entrepreneur, Educationist, Business Mentor

Agnelorajesh Athaide is a first-generation entrepreneur and works globally in the sectors of technology and education, concept-based real estate development, hospitality, sports and e-commerce. His offices are in India, Dubai and London.

Along with Lina Ashar (founder of Kangaroo Kids and Billabong High), he has embarked on a journey on maximising human potential through Korroboree (a gathering of sacred people for common good). Their mission is to develop habits of mind and parenting across the world and are in the process of building a school in Goa that will be unique in nature and methodology.

He is also developing real estate projects in and around Mumbai, Chennai, Madurai and Coimbatore. He has a keen interest in e-commerce and is heavily investing his money, time, efforts and knowledge in it. His online marketplace LifeStyleSasha’s objective is to promote and create a market for designers in the space of apparel, jewellery, accessories, home decor and art. He will also be investing in web series for OTT platforms.

He has invested in fashion brands www.deepthibalagiri.com ,www.ziyazyda.com and a jewellery online marketplace called Sashaisha which hosts two sub-brands, Mystique and KraKar. His objective is the global growth of these brands.


He created the platform of Global Indian Realty Summit in association with Network 18, a Mukesh Ambani enterprise. All episodes are posted on YouTube. He is also the chief mentor and co-founder of www.businessopportunities.club, a platform for helping entrepreneurs jump to the next level and change the mindset for growth.

He is the President of the Corporate Connections, Mumbai chapter, and director on the board of the Christian Chamber of Commerce and Industry. www.missingpeopleinfo.com and www.inspiringconversations.in are his social initiatives.

Life is a beautiful journey and a gift by god. He was invited by JOSH talks for narrating his business life. The Times of India has published a book on his life and the name of the book is Obstacles to Opportunities which is available on Amazon. The link below explains it all. https://youtu.be/tvQY3w2MfJs He loves to travel, and his wealth is the friendships that he has built across the world.
